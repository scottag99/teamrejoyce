Who We Are

Team ReJoyce’s annual Weekend of Hope will be held Saturday, April 30th at the Moulton City Park and Pavilion. This year, 100% of proceeds raised will benefit Candlelighters Childhood Cancer Family Alliance located in Houston, Texas, specifically their Parent Consultant Program.

The Parent Consultant Program is the core of the Candlelighters platform and provides for families during their toughest hours and days. All services provided to families are free of charge and include:

  • Emotional support
  • Parking Assistance
  • Meal passes
  • Toiletries
  • Grocery, Gas, and Pharmacy gift cards
  • Funeral assistance
  • Parent support group meetings

Join us April 30th to help raise money to provide for families with children with cancer. Candlelighters partners with families immediately upon diagnosis to help them through the emotional and financial burdens as they fight the disease and Team ReJoyce wants your help to build this community of support. For additional information about the Candlelighters Alliance, please visit candle.org.
